About Metal Detector

Metal Detector is a simple and intuitive app that uses the magnetometer in your Android phone to detect metals. With a user-friendly interface, this app makes metal detection accessible to anyone with a smartphone.

Metal Detector was developed by AppEDit Mobile, a team of talented developers dedicated to creating innovative and useful mobile applications. If you have any questions or feedback about the app, please don't hesitate to contact the developer at appeditmobile@gmail.com.

Please note that this app is not meant to replace a real metal detector. The magnetometer in mobile phones is not powerful enough or specifically designed to detect metals, and thus the results obtained from this app may not be 100% reliable. However, it can still be used to find metal objects that are lost or missing in dark spaces.
